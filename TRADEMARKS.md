## Acceptable Uses

You may use the Butter Vote name truthfully to refer to the original project, for example:

- "Forked from Butter Vote"
- "Compatible with Butter Vote"
- "Based on Butter Vote, licensed under AGPL-3.0-only"

You may preserve copyright, license, and attribution notices required by the AGPL-3.0 and NOTICE file.

## Public Forks And Modified Deployments

If you publicly deploy a modified version, please make it clear that it is a modified or unofficial version unless you have written permission to present it as official.

Public modified deployments should avoid using the Butter Vote logo or official trade dress as the primary identity of the service. A truthful attribution link to the original project is welcome.

These trademark guidelines are intended to prevent confusion about official identity and endorsement. They are not intended to restrict the rights granted by the AGPL-3.0 for the source code itself.
